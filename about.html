OUR SPECIALITY



Wedding Cakes


Any of the wedding cakes in our collection below can be customized to suit your theme, colour and size requirements. For couples looking for a completely bespoke wedding cake, we also offer a personalized design service, taking inspiration from your event, to create a truly stunning ‘one-of-a kind’ culinary masterpiece. All of our wedding cakes are made completely from scratch in our kitchen. We strongly believe all wedding Cakes should taste as good as they look and we pride ourselves on using only the highest quality ingredients in our recipes.



OUR SPECIAL TASTE



Miniature Cakes


Miniature cakes really are delightful little works of art, made up of small pieces of cake and layered with a sweet buttercream or rich chocolate ganache, then iced and decorated to your requirements. We have a minimum order of 8 cakes per design, and prices start from ₹799 per cake. Miniature cakes prices will vary on the size, (usually around 2.5 – 3inches) and amount of detail you require.
